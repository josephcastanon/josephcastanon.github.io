JACOB HILL

EAST VILLAGE, NY



“Where did your club name come from?”

I like the irony of using my own name.


"What was your most magical night out?"

My first night out in NY. It was at On Top and I met a ton of the friends I still keep today. There's just nothing like a rooftop party full of queens outside in NY.


“Describe yourself in 3 words.”

Creative, goofy, deliberate.


"What are your other interests outside of the club?"

Drawing, painting, graphic design, being in nature.


"Who is your biggest inspiration?"

I've admired Amanda Lepore ever since my manager at MAC in college showed me a video where she gets covered in Candy Yum Yum. Such a brave trailblazer.
