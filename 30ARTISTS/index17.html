JO



EAST VILLAGE, NYC



JO is the creator of The Kids Project and all photos for The Kids Project were taken by him at Kayvon Zand's "Metropolis".

“Where did your club name come from?”

My birth name is Joseph, but I've always gone by Joe. In high school, I had to do a project where I had to choose an emotion to correspond to each letter of my first name and I couldn't think of anything for the letter "e" so I just went with JO and it kind of stuck since. JO is more ambigious than Joe and I like to be as enigmatic as possible.

"What was your most magical night out?"

There's been so many magical nights, but there is nothing quite like the magic and the feeling that comes along with going out in a look for the first time. The first time I went out was to On Top and it was such a liberating experience and fulfilled so many of the dreams and expectations I had for the city.

“Describe yourself in 3 words.”

Disco Dumpster Clown.

"What are your other interests outside of the club?"

I'm currently studying Communication Design at Parsons The New School for Design and am constantly building my photography, art, and design work. I've lived passed lives as a working child actor, performer, singer/songwriter, and now... I'm a sparkly clown who only comes out at night. I like stepping out of my comfort zone and challenging myself in new fields of art, design & entertainment.. Thinking about taking up juggling next.

"Who is your biggest inspiration?"

Andy Warhol. He is everything I long to be and aspire towards. I ask myself "What would Warhol do?" everytime I have to make decisions and I will continue to try to bring the Warholian spirit of art, design, collaboration, and publication back into NYC queer nightlife.
