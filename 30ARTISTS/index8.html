LINUX

BROOKLYN, NY



“Where did your club name come from?”

I am named Linux after the computer operating system. It's super popular and quick software, however it's known to glitch ALOT and be a bit problematic...malware ensues.


"What was your most magical night out?"

My first night at Susanne Bartsch's ON TOP! It was also my first party I've ever been to in NYC. The venue, the people, the looks, and energy I felt that night is why I am still in Nightlife!


“Describe yourself in 3 words.”

Tall, Wide, Loud.


"What are your other interests outside of the club?"

I love studying PR & consumerism culture. It's super entertaining to me what the media thinks our mainstream minds want to see. I can spend all day watching a shitty reality show or researching a moment in popular culture, where actually I'm so into studying deeper details of what made those PR moments so iconic.


"Who is your biggest inspiration?"

My biggest inspiration is ever changing. One minute it's someone in nightlife, the next it's a fashion designer, a week later an author. Right now it is Amanda Lepore. She embodies all three of those things as well as beauty, being a transgender pioneer & existing in NYC nightlife for multiple decades.
