MALESTIA CHILD

BROOKLYN, NY



“Where did your club name come from?”

Back when I was coming up with names, I knew I wanted to have a one that was gross. One of my friends and I would get really stoned and spitball drag names back and forth, and Malestia Daughters somehow came up so I figured the last name should be Child, just to be an equally opportunity offender. Big Peg was another I was quite fond of.


"What was your most magical night out?"

Right around when I first started going out in looks, I was headed to On Top (of course) and we had to get out of our car like a block and a half early because there was construction. This couple walks out of some bar and the guy asks if he and his wife could take a picture with us. Everyone involved pretty drunk and we start talking with them. He introduced himself, and then his wife says "I'm Mel" in a think Leeds accent (which I only know because of her). Upon hearing her Leeds accent I blurt out "like Spice Girls Mel?" Which she nodded yes to. We ended up sitting on some shop stoop and talking for like an hour and she bummed half a pack of cigs from my friend. She was inspiring to me as a kid, so that was a pretty magical experience.


“Describe yourself in 3 words.”

Colorful, loud, lewd.


"What are your other interests outside of the club?"

I've recently gotten into gardening, which is rally just me buying plants and keeping most of them alive.


"Who is your biggest inspiration?"

I think I'll go with Tim Burton out of my countless people and things that I'm inspired by, because I think being obsessed with Nightmare Before Christmas at a very young age really set a certain trajectory for the evolution of my visual style.
