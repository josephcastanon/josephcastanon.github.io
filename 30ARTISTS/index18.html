CELERY

GREENWICH VILLAGE, NYC



“Where did your club name come from?”

In elementary school, kids used to tease me and call me Celery because I guess thats all they could come up with. So I kinda "took back the night" and now I use it to brand myself.


"What was your most magical night out?"

The most magical, I think, was the first time I ever went out. It was one of Susanne Bartsch’s parties, and I think I met you and Josh there too, but Milk performed and I even met Sharon Needles.


“Describe yourself in 3 words.”

Listener, unstable, and background.


"What are your other interests outside of the club?"

My other interests are pretty much fashion, and clothing related things. Other than obsessing about certain people, I guess I’m interested in health and wellness too.


"Who is your biggest inspiration?"

My biggest inspiration is John Waters, because he was the first person that ever made me feel normal, or at least not alone. I owe him like everything, coming to New York, starting fashion, starting drag...
