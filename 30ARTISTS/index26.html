HEATHER JONES

BRONX, NY



"Where did your club name come from?"

The critically-acclaimed film "Heathers" along side being obsessed with 80's/90's models. I decided on the ever-so androgynous Grace Jones. Clash them together and you get this wannabe secret agent runway assassin Heather Jones.


"What was your most magical night out?"

My first night ever performing (by a former alias of course), because I ended up getting kicked out. Hence the former alias.


“Describe yourself in 3 words.”

Love, Laughter, and Glamour.


"What are your other interests outside of the club?"

All of my interests include anything with beauty and opulence. If you're asking how I make a coin, I am a make up artist to the deceased.


"What is your biggest inspiration?"

Death. Death inspires me to keep loving and make choices that make me happy enough to know I will transition in Love, Laughter, and Glamour.
