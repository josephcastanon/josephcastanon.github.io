LUXURY-CARCASS

BROOKLYN, NY



“Where did your club name come from?”

The name I'm currently going by (luxurycarcass) is supposed to be a contradiction. I'm really interested in juxtapositions, and how different concepts interact with one another cohesively. This is something I always try to touch on in my art.


"What was your most magical night out?"

That's tough to say, but probably one of the many nights at Susanne Bartsch's seasonal party OnTop. It's a perfect setting for hot summer nights, and nobody tops Susanne's style of throwing extravaganzas.


“Describe yourself in 3 words.”

Ephemeral, unbiased, and unpredictable.


"What are your other interests outside of the club?"

Outside of looks, I create visual art (drawing, painting, and installation), and am currently training to be a makeup artist / esthetician. I would also like to get back into performance art in the near future.


"Who is your biggest inspiration?"

I definitely have countless influences and interests; however, I don't believe my art necessarily derives from anyone in particular. It is more or less a visual representation of my inner thoughts and feelings; an exaggerated yet pure version of my true self. My intention behind everything I do is exist vividly outside of any and all imposed margins.
