CHIMERA

GREENWICH VILLAGE, NYC



“Where did your club name come from?”

I chose Chimera because it’s a greek mythological creature. I’ve always been interested in shapeshifters, like Nue in Japanese mythology. I think it goes with the idea of being a nightlife personality. It only makes sense if I named myself after something that’s shapeshifting.


"What was your most magical night out?"

Honestly, even tho it’s super fun to serve a full look, most of the time I have more fun if i’m just beat and gorgeous. I love the idea of going to a club and just make out with complete strangers. I also love the nights where you meet some great people.


“Describe yourself in 3 words.”

Ethereal, Esoteric, Eccentric.


"What are your other interests outside of the club?"

I am currently working on my fashion design degree so being a club kid actually derived from my designs. I also love playing around with makeup.


"Who is your biggest inspiration?"

Berlin Queens! They are all so gorgeous from head to toe. They are like people from a different planet. Isshe Hungry is absolutely inspiring cuz I also love to play with the proportion of my face when painting
