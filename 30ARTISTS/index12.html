TYGA TONIC

BROOKLYN, NYC



“Where did your club name come from?”

My club name is a nickname I've had my entire life. When I started working in night life it seemed to fit as my name so I kept it.


"What was your most magical night out?"

I wouldn't say that there is one night in particular that stood out over the rest. To me, an amazing night is defined by those around me. I love seeing all of my friends hanging out in great looks, having fun and enjoying the party.


“Describe yourself in 3 words.”

fine, fierce, drunk


"Who is your biggest inspiration?"

My biggest inspirations would have to be my friends. At first I was getting all of my inspo through instagram, following an array of different artists, but once I got established in nightlife it was way more inspiring seeing my friends out every night continuously coming up with amazing, challenging looks. It makes me feel like I need to keep up with everyone!
