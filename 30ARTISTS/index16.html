NAY CAMPBELL



CHELSEA, NYC



“Where did your club name come from?”

My obsession with Andy Warhol and Naomi Campbell, but one of my girls started calling me Nay Nay campbell and it just kind of stuck.

"What was your most magical night out?"

I usually don't remember my nights so perfectly but it was probably the first time I saw Kembra Phaler perform, at Holy Mountain.

“Describe yourself in 3 words.”

Hectic, Loving, Inclusive.

"What are your other interests outside of the club?"

Stylist, designer, creative direction, memes.

"Who is your biggest inspiration?"

I have so many but probably one of them being Holly Golightly, not Hollywood's version, but the original character from Truman Capote. She's complex and inspiring, the true character represented a lot of girls and a generation at that.
